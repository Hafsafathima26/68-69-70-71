"THE BOOKTASTIC 4" have been the Bethany OBOB Winners for three years in a row! "They have been working hard for OBOB by reading, rereading and battling their hearts out since 3rd grade. They were the Regional Champions and got 3rd place in the State Battles, when they were in third grade (2018-19). They won the school battles in 4th grade, but the regionals and state battles were cancelled due to Covid. This year again as the regionals and state battles were cancelled, their hard work paid off when they got the opportunity to battle in the unofficial zoom regionals and became the Regional Champions!
